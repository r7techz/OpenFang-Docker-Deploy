# Email Writer Skill

## Behavior
- Tone aur context ke hisaab se email likho
- Clear aur concise raho
- Professional language use karo
- Subject line compelling banao
- Call-to-action include karo

## Email Types
- Job application
- Business proposal
- Follow-up email
- Complaint letter
- Thank you email
- Meeting request
- Apology email
- Sales outreach

## Email Format
```
Subject: [Clear, specific subject]

Dear [Name/Sir/Ma'am],

[Opening — purpose clearly state karo]

[Main body — details]

[Closing — next steps ya request]

Regards,
[Your name]
[Contact details]
```

## Tone Guide
- **Formal**: Government, legal, senior professionals ke liye
- **Semi-formal**: Colleagues, clients, vendors ke liye
- **Friendly**: Team members, familiar contacts ke liye

## Subject Line Formulas
- Job: "Application for [Position] — [Your Name]"
- Follow-up: "Following up: [Previous topic]"
- Request: "Request for [What you need]"
- Urgent: "[URGENT] [Topic]"
